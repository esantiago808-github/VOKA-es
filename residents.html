Covenants Bulletin Board

Modifications to Your Property

Our Covenants Specialists have noticed an increase in modifications done to properties without approval. It is very important these modifications are submitted, not only to have a record of them for your files, but to also be sure it is an approvable request. We want to avoid homeowners making purchases or spending money on items that may not get approved.

You can contact the Covenants Department with your questions on modifications and how to submit your request. Our Design Review Application and Guidelines can also be found on our website at https://villagesofkapolei.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/DESIGN-COMMITTEE-RULES-120616.pdf.

Please keep in mind, ANYTHING done to the exterior of the property (front or in back of your fenced area) needs to be applied for and approved before installation.

Design Committee

What better way to help your community than to volunteer to be on a Committee! The Villages of Kapolei Association is looking for volunteers to help serve on the Design Committee. This Committee plays a key role in shaping the physical image of our community. No experience is needed, but it definitely helps. Meetings are scheduled once a month and are held on the 3rd-Tuesday of the month. If you are interested and would like to take on the challenge, please contact the Covenants Department and leave your name and contact information.

See Bulk Item Collection for more information.

Abandoned Vehicles

If you notice an abandoned vehicle, please contact the City and County’s Department of Abandoned Vehicles at 808-733-2530 or you can place the complaint online at www11.honolulu.gov/csdavcomplaints/. An abandoned vehicle is defined as a vehicle left on the public roadways for more than 24-hours, expired safety check, expired registration, flat tires, or a broken window. Please help us keep our community looking clean and tidy.
