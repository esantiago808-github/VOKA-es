Meetings on the last Monday of every month
6:30 pm
Rec 2 (Mauka Hall) - 91-1050 Kama'aha Loop

NeighborhoodWatch Meetings are held at 6:30 p.m., on the last Monday of every month over at Rec 2. Our Community HPD Officer will be there with Kapolei’s crime statistics for the past month and to talk story with our community. We share any suspicious activity, progress on the previous month’s issues, and any other points of concern in the neighborhood. We encourage people to come to these meetings and get involved. We want each village to eventually start their own walking citizen patrol, so we need you!


Honolulu 311 Application


Honolulu 311 is the official mobile application for the City & County of Honolulu. You can report non-emergencies and track the status of your service request. Report potholes, broken streetlights, cracked sidewalks, illegal dumping, vandalized/broken signs and more via your smartphone. A picture tells a thousand words and the Honolulu 311 app makes it a snap. Download it today!
