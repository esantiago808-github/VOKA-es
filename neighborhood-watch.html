Honolulu 311 Application

Honolulu 311 is the official mobile application for the City & County of Honolulu. You can report nonemergencies and track the status of your service request. Report potholes, broken streetlights, cracked sidewalks, illegal dumping, vandalized/broken signs and more via your smartphone. A picture tells a thousand words and the Honolulu 311 app makes it a snap. Download it today!
