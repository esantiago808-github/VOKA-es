BreaksR4Kidz Hip Hop

(Coming Soon)
Days: Tuesday &Thursdays
Time: 4:00 – 6:30 p.m.
Rec 1
Fee: TBD
A fun hip hop class that teaches youth about Hawaii’s hip hop culture

Breaks R4 Kidz is a dance program that inspires children and adults by upholding the true principles of hip hop culture. Their principles are peace, love, unity and safety while having fun. Their mission is to help students maintain a better understanding of themselves and their potential through breakdancing and the hip hop culture.

This type of performance art is an inexpensive activity that promotes a healthy lifestyle and mental well-being. Positive reinforcement is used in all lessons and students are encouraged to pass on the positive vibes to their peers.Instructors feel that when children are given the responsibility of motivating their classmates, there is a better sense of unity within the learning environment. The effect is to create a comfortable setting which allows children to open up more when they know that the lesson is a group effort and not a competition.

Before and after class, the children shake each other’s hands to show respect. Showing respect is an important part of breakdancing because the dancers share a common bond through the love of hip hop, and this love supersedes any competing negativity.

There is a misconception on the issue of safety in breakdancing. It is possible that dancers may get hurt doing particular moves. What most breakdancing injuries have in common is the lack of preparation.People get hurt because they attempt to do dance moves that are advanced without learning the basics first. Every advanced dance move in the book has evolved from at least 2-3 basic foundations. Students are trained thoroughly through the basics before trying anything advanced.
