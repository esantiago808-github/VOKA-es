Recreational Programs


Fitness


Aqua Jogging

Days: Mon, Wed, Fri
Time: 9:00 - 10:00 a.m.
Fee: Free
An informal exercise group at the pool. Water exercise improves circulation, lowers blood pressure, and increases range of motion without the stress of gravity

Kickboxing

Days: Mondays & Wednesdays
Time: 4:00 p.m.
Rec. 1
Fee: $40.00 per month
Ages: 8 and up
Instructor Derrick Bright has over 20 years of teaching experience in the martial arts, with an emphasis on kickboxing. Focus on discipline, self respect and good health while enjoying agility and cardio workouts.

Orbix Fitness

Mondays, 8:00 - 9:00 p.m.
Rec 2
Fee: $5.00 per class
A Tahitian style fitness class that will get you moving. Don’t forget to bring your water bottle and a pareo if you have one.

Yoga Plain & Simple

Days: Saturdays
Time: 8:00 - 9:00 a.m.
Rec. 1
Fee: $5.00 per class or $20.00 per month
Instructor: Harry Winfield
Increase flexibility, balance, alignment and strength. Bring a yoga mat, large towel, and bottled water.

Zumba & Pound Fitness Classes

Days: Mondays and most Wednesdays
Time: 7:00 - 8:30 p.m.
Rec. 1
Fee: $5.00 per class
Instructor: Anela Jackson
Dance your way to fitness with Zumba, then drum up a healthier you with Pound fitness. Pound uses lightly weighted exercise drumsticks to engage the whole body. Tone, sculpt and cardio the fun way.

Feel Good Stretch

Days: Tuesdays & Thursdays
Time: 5:00-5:50 p.m.
Rec. 1
Fee: $5.00 per class
Instructor: Anna Peahu
Adaptive fitness for individual success. Increase range of motion, balance, mobility and circulation.
Feel Good Stretch

Scouts

Club Scouts

Children in grades 1 - 5 enjoy activities which focus on developing character, improving fitness, teaching practical skills and community service. See their Facebook page @cubscoutpack126kapolei for more information.

Girl Scouts

Days: 2nd & 4th Tuesdays
Time: 5:30 p.m.
Rec. 1
Girls in grades K - 12 engage in skill-based badges, make friends, explore leadership opportunities, work collaboratively and serve their community. For more information contact gshwestside@gmail.com or visit us at https://www.gshawaii.org/.

Martial Arts

Karate

Days: Tuesdays & Thursdays
Time: 6:30 p.m.
Rec. 1
Fee: $40.00 per month
Combines physical and mental benefits such as self-discipline, respect, concentration and courtesy

Tropic Lightening Taekwondo

Days: Tuesday &Thursdays
Time: 5:00 - 7:00 p.m
Rec. 2
Fee: est. $90.00 per month (varies)
Martial arts skills with an emphasis on confidence, respect, discipline and character development. Free 2-week introductory trial.
Taekwondo

Keiki Steps

Days: Monday - Thursday
Time: 8:30 - 11:30 a.m.
Rec. 1
Ages: birth to 5, with a parent/adult.
This free parent participation preschool prepares children for school through a variety of developmentally and culturally appropriate activities. Register by calling 620-9043 or email info@inpeace.org.

Junior Swim Team

Days: Mondays,Wednesdays, Fridays
Time: 4:00 - 5:30 p.m.
Fee: $50.00 per month
Ages: 6 - 18
Stroke refinement, conditioning, discipline and exercise. Pre-test minimum: 100 yards continuous swimming with 3 out of 4 competitive strokes at a level 5/6 standard. Call the recreation office for more information 674-4444, dept. 2.

Seniors Club

Days: Thursdays
Time: 10:00 a.m.
Rec. 1
Fee: Free
Ages: 55 and older
Come and enjoy games, crafts, educational presentations and more.
